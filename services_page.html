24/7 Help Hotline

If you have suicidal thoughts or thoughts of harming others,
find yourself in a difficult situation in life be it financial hardship,
the death of a family member or health issues,
Please call us under (+00 123456789). You are not alone!

Emergency Counseling-Sessions

If you preffer face to face comunication we offer in-person, one on one counseling sessions
You will need to make an appointment with one of our 6 inhouse therapists.
We can provide a maximum of 2 counseling sessions of 45 minutes per quarter year to anny resident of Harbortown County Therapie Hourse are from 9am-3pm on weekdays.
We only offer in-person session in our downtown clinic!
Please call us under (+00 123456999) to make an appointment.
